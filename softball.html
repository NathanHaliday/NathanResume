Softball

I started softball in 2022, playing for the U15s Pokeno Razorbacks team, a team and club I have grown with and still cherish.

Softball is a sport I have grown to love, and I am always looking for ways to improve my skills and knowledge of the game. I have been involved in various roles, including playing, umpiring, and coaching.

My team, the Pokeno Razorbacks U15s, were thrown into a tough season, with many of our players being new to the game or with only a few years under their belt. The teams we played against were all very experienced, with many having all players with 10 years of experience. Although at the start of the season we were losing games, we never gave up and always looked for ways to improve, and by the end of the season we were ever so close to winning games, even the other teams were surprised at how much we improved. This could not have been done without our coach or the team spirit Pokeno is all about. I am very proud of my team and how far we have come, and I am looking forward to next season.

I will continue developing my skills and knowledge of the game, and I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow.
